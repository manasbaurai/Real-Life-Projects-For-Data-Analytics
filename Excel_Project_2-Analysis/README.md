# Job Role & Skills Salary Analysis – Excel Project

## Overview
In today’s competitive job market, researching a suitable job role can be overwhelming due to the sheer volume of information available online. Understanding which skills are required for a specific role and evaluating their earning potential often becomes a time-consuming and confusing process.

This Excel project is designed to simplify that research. It provides a focused, data-driven view of job roles, the skills required for them, and the average annual salaries associated with both the roles and the in-demand skills. By presenting only what truly matters, the project helps users make informed career decisions efficiently.

---

## Problem Statement
Job seekers often face the following challenges:
- Excessive and scattered information about job roles  
- Difficulty identifying the most relevant skills for a specific role  
- Lack of clear salary insights for both job roles and individual skills  
- Time-consuming manual research across multiple platforms  

These challenges make it harder to identify the best career path or skill set to focus on.

---

## Project Objective
The primary objective of this project is to:
- Streamline job role research  
- Highlight only the essential skills required for a chosen role  
- Provide clear salary insights to support informed career decisions  

---

- Displays only the relevant skills required for a selected job role
